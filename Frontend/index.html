About Us

The journals under BuildSoftTech Publication aims to provide a platform for researchers, academics, and professionals to publish high-quality and innovative research in the following domains:

• Computer Science Engineering: Topics include but are not limited to artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, software engineering, and cybersecurity.

• Mechanical Engineering: Topics such as robotics, thermodynamics, materials science, manufacturing processes, and automotive engineering.

• Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering: Areas including signal processing, wireless communication, embedded systems, and VLSI design.

• Civil Engineering: Research on structural engineering, environmental engineering, geotechnical engineering, and sustainable construction.

• General Engineering: Interdisciplinary topics that bridge multiple fields of engineering, innovations, and technology applications.
